For Artists

If you're an artist interested in showcasing your work in our gallery, we welcome submissions of all forms of art, including photography, painting, and digital art.

Submission Guidelines:

  • Prepare a portfolio showcasing your artwork. This can include high-resolution images or digital samples of your work.
  • Include a brief artist statement or bio describing yourself and your artistic vision.
  • Provide any additional documentation or information relevant to your artwork.
  • Submit your portfolio and relevant materials via email to submissions@shadesofsorrowgallery.com.

Our curatorial team will review your submission and get in touch with you regarding potential opportunities for exhibition.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of art do you exhibit in your gallery?

We exhibit a wide range of art forms, including photography, painting, digital art, sculpture, and mixed media. Our goal is to provide a diverse and eclectic collection that appeals to a broad audience.

How can I submit my artwork for consideration?

If you're an artist interested in showcasing your work in our gallery, please refer to our submission guidelines on the Contact page. You can submit your portfolio and relevant materials via email to submissions@shadesofsorrowgallery.com.

Do you accept submissions from international artists?

Yes, we welcome submissions from artists worldwide. We believe in celebrating diverse perspectives and cultures through art and encourage artists from all backgrounds to submit their work for consideration.

How often do you rotate your exhibitions?

We strive to keep our exhibitions fresh and engaging by rotating them regularly. The frequency of rotations may vary depending on the theme and duration of each exhibition, but we aim to introduce new artworks and artists to our audience on a regular basis.
